## Different Products Need Different Image Strategies

Portrait enhancement means different things in different products.

A selfie camera needs fast preview and an instantly pleasing look. A social profile app needs natural, believable portraits. E-commerce images need consistency. Event photography needs better output under difficult lighting and sometimes batch-friendly processing.

Chroma Beauty SDK can be evaluated as a set of modules that combine AI color grading, portrait protection, beauty retouching, style controls and export workflow.

## Use Case 1: Beauty Camera and Selfie Tools

For selfie products, the goal is simple: users want to look better immediately, but they do not want to look fake.

Useful modules include:

- one-tap color enhancement;
- skin smoothing and whitening;
- rosy tone and shine control;
- face shaping and facial feature controls;
- before / after comparison;
- style presets for different portrait moods.

![Beauty Controls](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/18818474455/ChromaBeautySDK/main/assets/apk-demo/beauty-shape-controls.jpg)

## Use Case 2: Social, Dating and Avatar Products

For social and dating products, over-processing can reduce trust. The goal is a clean and attractive portrait that still feels natural.

Recommended direction:

| Module | Product Goal |
|---|---|
| Portrait protection | Keep skin tone stable |
| Light smoothing | Improve skin without losing realism |
| Color enhancement | Make the image cleaner and more shareable |
| Style presets | Let users choose different moods quickly |
| Export consistency | Keep upload output close to preview |

![Style Panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/18818474455/ChromaBeautySDK/main/assets/apk-demo/style-panel.jpg)

## Use Case 3: E-commerce Portraits and Model Images

E-commerce images need visual consistency. Images captured in different locations, lighting conditions, and devices can look inconsistent across a product page or campaign.

Chroma can support:

- skin-tone consistency for portrait images;
- background and portrait-aware enhancement;
- protection for white clothing and highlight areas;
- brand style presets;
- post-processing for model images, campaign images and product detail pages.

![Color Panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/18818474455/ChromaBeautySDK/main/assets/apk-demo/color-panel.jpg)

## Use Case 4: Wedding, Event and Conference Photos

Event photos often include difficult lighting: LED screens, stage lights, mixed indoor light, busy backgrounds and group portraits.

A practical workflow can be:

```text
Photo input
-> AI color recommendation
-> Background atmosphere enhancement
-> Portrait protection
-> Manual fine-tuning
-> Export
```

![Demo Flow](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/18818474455/ChromaBeautySDK/main/assets/apk-demo/demo-flow.gif)

## Use Case 5: AI Image Post-processing

AI-generated images often need final tuning before they are used in a product. Common issues include inconsistent skin tones, overly sharp local details, unstable style, or unnatural portrait areas.

Chroma can be used as a post-processing layer for:

- AI color normalization;
- style unification;
- portrait enhancement;
- skin-area polishing;
- export preparation.
